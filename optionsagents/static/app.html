Market scanner

No watchlist needed — the scanner finds candidates across the whole market by itself. Every 5 minutes during market hours it pulls the most active names, biggest gainers and losers, and trending tickers from free market data, filters out illiquid ones, runs EMA/VWAP + EMA/RSI entry rules, and lets the AI brain rank and pick the best setups. Every entry is sized to your risk % from the Account tab and auto-closed on target, stop, or expiry.
